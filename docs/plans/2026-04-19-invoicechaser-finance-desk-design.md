Invoice Chaser redesign direction: calmer finance ops desk.

Goals:
- remove the older light admin-dashboard feel
- align uploads, saved documents, queue review, and draft editing with the dark Labs shell
- keep risk and collections context legible without pushing the interface into a loud command-center aesthetic

Visual direction:
- restrained dark surfaces with softer contrast and clearer spacing
- intake and queue status positioned as the primary top-level workflow
- saved documents and recent activity treated as quieter supporting rails
- draft editing framed as an operational writing workspace rather than a utility form

Implementation notes:
- preserve all existing upload, queue, draft, and activity-log behavior
- keep the current auth gate flow for uploads
- restyle risk badges, notices, summary cards, and queue items into calmer finance-oriented components
